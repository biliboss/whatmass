Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Masswhats

Is Masswhats compliant with WhatsApp's terms of service?

Yes, Masswhats operates within WhatsApp's guidelines. We use official WhatsApp Business API to ensure your account remains in good standing while sending mass messages.

How many messages can I send per day?

During the beta phase, you can send unlimited messages. Our system is designed to send messages at a natural pace to avoid any restrictions from WhatsApp. We recommend starting with smaller batches and gradually increasing volume.

Is Masswhats really free?

Yes, Masswhats is completely free during our beta phase. We may introduce paid plans in the future, but all beta users will receive special pricing options and advance notice before any changes.

Do I need to install any software?

No, Masswhats is a cloud-based solution. You only need to connect your WhatsApp account through our secure web interface, and you're ready to start sending messages.

How do I connect my WhatsApp account?

Simply enter the 6-digit sync code (233-928) in your WhatsApp app. Open WhatsApp on your phone, go to Settings > Linked Devices > Link a Device, and enter the code. The connection is secure and takes less than 30 seconds.

Can I include images or videos in my messages?

Absolutely! Masswhats supports all media types that WhatsApp allows, including images, videos, documents, and voice messages. Rich media can significantly increase engagement rates.
